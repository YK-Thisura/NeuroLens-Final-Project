Brain Tumor Detection and Care with Cutting Edge AI and Healthcare Solutions

Our Brain Tumor Detection and Classification App leverages cutting-edge AI and machine learning to analyze MRI scans, identifying tumor types like Glioma, Meningioma, Pituitary, and No Tumor. Once the scan is uploaded, the model provides an immediate result.

The app suggests personalized treatment options while ensuring patient privacy. The "Meet Your Doctor" feature allows specialists to review and adjust the treatment plan. This web app streamlines the diagnostic process, providing timely and secure access to treatment recommendations, saving time for both patients and doctors.
